### 5. Race Day Integrity & Horse Welfare
**Context:** Scrutiny over race day integrity and horse welfare remains a critical area. Mentions of ground conditions affecting participation (e.g., Constitution Hill) and serious health issues (e.g., laminitis claiming Frosted) further emphasize the industry's focus on horse well-being and fair competition.
