### Zac Purton's Historic Hong Kong Milestone
**Facts:**
Jockey **Zac Purton** has achieved a monumental career milestone, becoming the first jockey to ride 2,000 winners in Hong Kong. This feat, accomplished over 19 years, has seen him accumulate over 100 million in prize money. The achievement has been widely celebrated across Hong Kong and international racing media, highlighting his enduring dominance and legacy in the region. His personal life and partnership with Nicole Purton, as well as his connection to horses like **Ka Ying Rising**, are also subjects of interest.

This statement brings a critical social commentary to the forefront of racing discourse.
