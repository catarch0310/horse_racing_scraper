### 1.4 Regulatory and Governance Challenges

**Facts:**
The racing industry faces ongoing challenges in governance and regulation across multiple jurisdictions. In Australia, Racing NSW's power to appoint an ATC administrator was upheld by the Court of Appeal, leading to calls for the current ATC board to step down. In the US, HISA continues to evolve with new appointments to its Veterinary Advisory Committee and a general counsel retirement. The NYSGC Panel is reviewing fire prevention infrastructure, and Maryland bought out Churchill's interest in the Preakness, indicating significant structural changes. Ireland saw Shark Hanlon's fine for verbal abuse increased tenfold by the IHRB, highlighting disciplinary actions. The broader issue of affordability checks and the black market (as noted in Betting Industry section) also falls under this regulatory umbrella, with regulators struggling to gain control.
